Reception Hall

Our reception hall is a beautiful ball room that can accomidate up to 250 people. You can bring your own decorations or you can rent our decorations that come in a limited variety of colors. If you choose to use our decorations we will be happy to set up the hall for you! We also offer a limited variety of catering options if you don't want to provide your own food. If you would like to hire outside catering or cater yourself we have a kitechen availible for your use! If you have questions or concerns please fill out the provided form and we will get back to you as soon as possible!
