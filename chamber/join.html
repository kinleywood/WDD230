Membership Benefits

Non Profit Membership

  • Integragte yourself into the commumity and build relationships.
  • Company Name on Chamber website.
  • Get the chance to tell your story to the community.
  • Participate on our community fundraisers.
  • Member rates at all chamber events.

Bronze Membership

  • Includes all non profit benefits.
  • Best for small or medium sized businesses
  • Marketing and promotional exposure.
  • Register up to three representatives or employees as members.

Silver Membership

  • Includes all non profit and bronze benefits.
  • Best for medium sized businesses.
  • Personalized business introductions.
  • Invitation to chairmans circle for all representatives.
  • Invitation for company representatives to attend Chamber Board dinners and receptions

Gold Membership

  • Includes all non profit, bronze, and silver benefits.
  • May be considered to become a guest speaker at chamber events.
  • Introduction to Chamber board durring in-person Board meetings.
